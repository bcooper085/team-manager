About Cougar Golf

Why choose The Cougar Golf Academy?

  1. Great Instruction: Campers will receive instruction from top Collegiate Coaches, Collegiate Players, and High School Coaches

  2. Advance Technology: Campers will be videoed with High Definition Cameras and will receive full swing, short game, and putting analysis.

  3. Learn new techniques for player development: Campers will leave with individual game plans for player development which will include Aimpoint Green Reading Techniques, Short Game Development, and TPI Fitness.

  4. Great gift for the avid Seattle Golf Fan.

  5. The Cougar Golf Academy Day Camp is held at the exclusive Golf Club of Seattle.
